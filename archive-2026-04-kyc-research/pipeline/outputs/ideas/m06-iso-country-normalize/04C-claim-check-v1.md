## Kherson / Zaporizhzhia NOT comprehensively sanctioned

- **Claim:** v1 says these are not in OFAC's comprehensively-sanctioned territory set as of late 2025/early 2026.
- **PASS.** Search-result excerpt explicitly states: "OFAC has issued guidance stating that Executive Order 14065 targets only the DNR, LNR and other Covered Regions determined by the Secretary of the Treasury... The disputed Kherson and Zaporizhzhia regions have not been sanctioned by the United States." This is the correct, current legal posture.
- **Caveat:** "as of late 2025/early 2026" — best guess based on most recent search results; this status could change without notice if Treasury makes a new determination under EO 14065. The v1 already flags this dynamism.
