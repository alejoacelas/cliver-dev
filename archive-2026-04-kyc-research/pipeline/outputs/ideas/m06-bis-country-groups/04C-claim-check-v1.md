# 4C claim check — m06-bis-country-groups v1

## BIS Country Groups interactive page

- **URL:** https://www.bis.gov/regulations/ear/interactive-country-groups
- **PASS.** Canonical BIS-hosted interactive Country Groups page (Supplement No. 1 to Part 740). Search results confirm this is BIS's official interactive tool.

## BIS Commerce Country Chart interactive page

- **URL:** https://www.bis.gov/regulations/ear/interactive-commerce-country-chart
- **PASS.** Canonical BIS-hosted interactive Commerce Country Chart (Supplement No. 1 to Part 738).

## No JSON API for Country Chart

- **Claim:** No machine-readable feed exists.
- **Verification:** ITA's Trade.gov developer portal lists the Consolidated Screening List API and several other trade APIs, but the Country Chart and Country Groups are not among them. Searched listings on developer.trade.gov and the Commerce Data Hub. **PASS.**

## Nixon Peabody May 2024 alert

- **URL:** https://www.nixonpeabody.com/insights/alerts/2024/05/10/bis-updates-commerce-country-chart-and-makes-conforming-changes-to-the-ear
- **PASS.** Real client-alert URL from a major export-controls practice covering the May 2024 BIS Country Chart restructure.

## BIS BioExport guidance

- **URL:** https://www.bis.doc.gov/index.php/documents/product-guidance/1107-bioexport-pdf/file
- **PASS.** Long-standing BIS guidance for life-science exporters; covers Country Chart application to biological items.

## 15 CFR 762.6 retention

- **Claim:** 5-year recordkeeping retention.
- **PASS.** 15 CFR § 762.6(a) requires records of export transactions to be retained for 5 years from the latest of several trigger events. This is a stable, well-known regulation.

## E:1 / E:2 country list

- **Claim:** Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria are in E:1/E:2.
- **Verification:** Per Supplement No. 1 to Part 740 as published in the EAR, E:1 currently lists Iran, North Korea, Syria, Cuba (terrorist-supporting / unilateral embargo). **PASS** — current as of late 2025/early 2026.
- **Note on Russia/Belarus:** v1 says "de-facto under sectoral measures" rather than placing them in E. This is correct: post-2022 controls put Russia/Belarus under heightened licensing under Part 746 sectoral rules and Country Group D additions, NOT formal E:1/E:2 listing. Properly hedged in v1.

## Verdict

PASS. All citations resolve and substantively back the claims.
